Our Project in brief

Our goal is to digitize documents containing handwriting while respecting data confidentiality.

To do this, we used a new learning technology called federated learning, which allows the client not to share this data with the server.

For text recognition, we use a trained OCR model. In order to adapt it to our customers' data, we use transfer learning to simplify the learning process.
